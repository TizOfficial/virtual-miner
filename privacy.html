Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Virtual Miner:

  • Discord user ID and username
  • Game progress and statistics
  • Server settings and configurations
  • Command usage data

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing bot functionality
  • Storing user progress
  • Maintaining leaderboards
  • Improving bot performance

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely on protected servers. We implement appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

4. Data Retention

We retain user data for as long as the user remains active. Inactive users may have their data removed after 180 days of inactivity. Users can request data deletion at any time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our bot, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Request correction of inaccurate data

7. Cookies

Virtual Miner does not use cookies. All data is stored server-side and is only accessible through Discord interactions.

8. Third Party Services

We do not share data with third party services except as necessary to provide the bot's core functionality through Discord's API.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Discord: Join our support server
  • Email: support@virtualminer.com

11. Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights.

12. Compliance

We comply with Discord's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We also adhere to GDPR requirements for EU users and CCPA requirements for California residents.
